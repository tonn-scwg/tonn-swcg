Which one should you pick?

Electric

Requires an amplifier. Known as the most versatile type, it can produce a wide range of tones, from rock, pop, country, metal, and more. However, it is often more expensive than other guitars, so get this if you are starting to get more serious about your playing.

Acoustic

Recommended for beginners because it is affordable, does not require an amplifier, and helps strengthen your fingers. Since it is harder to play than an electric guitar (requiring more pressure for a clean sound), starting with an acoustic makes switching to electric much easier later on.

Classical

This guitar is more niche than the electric or the acoustic as it is designed mainly for classical music. It uses soft nylon strings which produce a warm, mellow tone common in classical music.
